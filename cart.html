About Care-I Tea

Care-I Tea is a wellness-focused powdered tea brand committed to providing convenient, health-conscious beverage options for everyday self-care.

Privacy Policy

Any data collected through this website, such as names, contact details, and order information, is used solely for order processing, customer service, and communication purposes. We do not sell, share, or disclose personal information to third parties without consent, except when required by law.

Sustainability Commitment

Care-I Tea is committed to promoting mindful consumption and environmental responsibility. We use eco-friendly, resealable packaging to reduce waste and preserve product quality. The brand supports sustainable sourcing practices and encourages customers to practice responsible disposal and reuse of packaging as part of a wellness-driven lifestyle.
